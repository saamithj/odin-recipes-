How to Cook Boiled Eggs

Hard boiled eggs make a rich, protein-packed snack, salad topping, or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • One to 12 eggs
  • One cup of cold water
  • One Instant Pot with its trivet

Steps

  1. Add eggs
  2. Pour one cup water
  3. Cook for five minutes
  4. Transfer the eggs and let them cool off
