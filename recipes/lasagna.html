Yummy Lasagna

This hearty, vegetable lasagna is the only lasagna my wife will eat. We love it!!! Hope you all enjoy as much as we do.

Ingredients

  • Package lasagna noodels
  • Fresh mushrooms sliced
  • Chopped green bell pepper
  • 2 jars pasta sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling water for 10 minutes
  2. n a large saucepan, cook and stir mushrooms, green peppers, onion, and garlic in oil.
  3. Mix together ricotta, 2 cups mozzarella cheese, and eggs.
  4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  5. Bake, uncovered, for 40 minutes. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
